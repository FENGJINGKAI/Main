Bothriocephalus scorpii (Müller, 1776)
中文名：无
|
Bothriocephalus scorpii is a species of flatworms in the family Bothriocephalidae. It is a parasite. Individuals can grow to 950 mm. Bothriocephalus scorpii is a hermaphrodite.
