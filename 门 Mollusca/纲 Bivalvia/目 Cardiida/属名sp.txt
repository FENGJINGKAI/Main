名称 Cardium
中文名 鸟蛤
|
The term cardium comes from the Greek word for 'heart'. It is a frequently encountered term in physiology where it is incorporated into scientific nomenclature. 
Cardium became the name of a taxonomic genus within the family Cardiidae – the cockles because of the heart-like appearance of its shells.
Their shells are widely used in cardium pottery as a decorative style imprint.
