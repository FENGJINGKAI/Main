Branchiobdellida is an order of freshwater leech-like clitellates that are obligate ectosymbionts or ectoparasites, 
mostly of astacoidean crayfish.[1] They are found in the Northern Hemisphere and have a holarctic distribution in East Asia,
the Euro-Mediterranean region and North and Central America, 
with the greatest species diversity being in North and Central America.
